Movement
--------

Numpad, arrow keys, or VI Keys:     Move

Shift-<move key>:                   Move until blocked

Ctrl-x:                             Autoexplore

; or Numpad 5:                      Rest for 1 turn

Shift-; or Shift-5:                 Rest for 100 turns

Ctrl-o:                             Manually open door

Ctrl-c:                             Manually close door

>:                                  Use stairs down

<:                                  Use stairs up


Item & Inventory
----------------

i:                                  Show inventory

g or ,:                             Get items

d:                                  Drop items

w:                                  Wield item

W:                                  Wear item

e:                                  Eat item


Misc
----

?:                                  Show this help screen
